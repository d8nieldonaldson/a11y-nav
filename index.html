Example of an accessible navigation menu with nested content

Requirements: keyboard navigation

  • Toggle submenu on parent item hover
  • Toggle submenu on button click
  • Close submenu by hitting ESC key
  • Close submenu by tabbing to next/previous focusable item
  • Close submenu by clicking outside of submenu
  • Navigate submenu items with UP and DOWN arrow keys
  • Submenu items must loop
